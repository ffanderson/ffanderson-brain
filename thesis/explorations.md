# Active Explorations

The queue of areas the owner is actively curious about. [[Davy]] appends
bullets when a thought asserts a new area; [[Glendower]] consumes them to
produce theme dossiers in `themes/`.

Lifecycle:
1. **Bullet here** — curious, want a corpus + web scan.
2. **Dossier in `themes/`** — Glendower has scouted. Owner reviews.
3. **Pillar in `thesis/pillars/`** — owner has conviction. The exploration
   becomes a position. Delete the bullet from this queue.
