### 2026-06-04 — Valuation trajectory via social capital

Starcloud was cited as a case study in how social capital and thought leadership can generate outsized valuation before technical reality matures. The company started as 'three guys with a single GPU' and reached a valuation described as around $2.2 billion after Elon Musk gave it favorable mentions on podcasts and [[Benchmark]] backed the round. The group noted that successfully launching even a modest payload on a Starship required navigating Musk's attention—a high social hurdle that most technically brilliant but isolated teams fail to clear.
