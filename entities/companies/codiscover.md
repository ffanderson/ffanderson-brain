### 2026-04-26 — Branching agent workspace for knowledge discovery
<!-- source_hash: dff89411b86d95696a760b2341513ae71c1279ce4d158f02104e1f636a1da7ec -->
CoDiscover presented as an AI-powered collaborative knowledge space for long-term research exploration. Its key differentiator is a branching workflow where users can spin off agent sub-branches mid-session to fetch papers or do focused work, then merge findings back into a main node. All notes, citations, and chat history are shareable in real time across accounts, and the system supports cross-referencing to arbitrary document fragments. The judges asked about its relationship to Elicit; the team argued their persistent, branching knowledge graph avoids the problem of agents dumping a large, unstructured paragraph with no further interaction surface.
