### 2026-04-01 — Current sub-accounting dependency and concerns

SS&C (referenced as SST/SNC/SS&C) currently handles trade reconciliation, break processing, dividend processing, and significant back-office operations for [[LPL Financial]], with much of the heavy daily recon occurring in Kansas City rather than at LPL. Charles expressed concern that unwinding from SS&C would require LPL to absorb substantial operational capability, noting that people from SS&C's dividend team are already joining the competing platform and describing the transition risk as 'the main thing.' The incumbent was also criticised for lacking integrated same-day settlement and for a manual T0 exception process not embedded in its core system.
