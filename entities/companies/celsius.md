Nick described Celsius—alongside [[Zutacore]]—as a leading pumped nucleate-boiling two-phase competitor that raised $65M from Johnson Controls and others. Both companies' approaches require 5–15°C of superheat before boiling initiates, consuming a large fraction of the total ~40°C thermal budget, and their systems face vapor lock risks, complex mixed-phase condenser management, and catastrophic failure modes if a pump stops.
