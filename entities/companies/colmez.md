### 2026-04-14 — Product: hazardous waste indices and swaps
<!-- source_hash: 1617bec6296efc9c33301874cce0467caf502bf9e104e72ddc94cef32baeeae5 -->
Colmez is building the first hazardous waste disposal index, offering cash-settled swaps and aiming to become a commodities exchange. Their initial index, the Northeastern Kiln Bulk Liquids, is segmented by geography and waste stream to enable localized price discovery. The core value proposition is converting opaque, volatile TSDF variable-rate contracts into fixed-rate arrangements for generators, with Colmez currently taking the balance sheet risk and earning a fee plus expectation spread.
