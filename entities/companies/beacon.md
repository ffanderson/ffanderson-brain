### 2026-04-01 — Business model and site portfolio
<!-- source_hash: 91eb914e1b6f9f37763810f5c40c953b8ae30449825d1977df98e454426972be -->
Beacon is a data center developer and operator that has grown incrementally by raising family office capital in tranches to acquire and develop land before raising at the asset level from institutional investors like [[Brookfield]], preserving significantly more equity than competitors who capitalize the full platform upfront. The company has sites in Alberta (two in Edmonton), New Brunswick, Ohio, San Angelo Texas, Alabama, and Bakersfield California, and recently hired the head of development from [[Stacks]] as it transitions from land-and-shell strategy to full vertical build-out driven by hyperscaler demand for turnkey facilities.
