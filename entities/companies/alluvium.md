### 2026-06-25 — Product vision: AI-native chemical software
<!-- source_hash: 70f959e6ef849a861de6c4a79e382a6e9dee2268a01514f0acc68ee37e534639 -->
Alluvium is building AI-native enterprise software for the chemical and manufacturing industries, targeting the deeply fragmented legacy software stack that dates from the 1980s and 1990s. The platform aims to unify the three operational layers of a chemical plant — plant design, process simulation, and controls engineering — which currently run on six or seven disconnected tools with no interoperability. The same fragmentation problem applies across petrochemicals, pharmaceuticals, and processed food manufacturing.
