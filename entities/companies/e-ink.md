### 2026-06-25 — Founders now on Eric's team
<!-- source_hash: d3352e67f6f2ddc11b3f6570a8e2d40fc6301d44d9bc5e3637422f988ff7dea1 -->
Barrett Kaminski and JD Albert, the co-inventors of E Ink technology developed at MIT, are working with [[Eric]] on his license plate frame venture. The two were inducted into the Inventor's Hall of Fame; the company they founded was sold for $400 million to a Taiwanese paper company, with the inventors receiving around $180,000. That acquirer still owns E Ink, which Eric described as carrying a market cap of around $13 billion today, with Amazon's Kindle as its largest customer.

### 2026-06-25 — License plate cover exclusive deal
<!-- source_hash: 7a9305725b5dfa36b9c4d7881a33702f800f66e5be848cd21db362ddad6b404d -->
E Ink, the display technology manufacturer behind the Kindle and the recently released color Kindle, granted [[Eric Doelle]] an exclusive category license to apply its color e-ink technology to license plate covers. Analogous commercial deployments mentioned include a plug-free picture frame product and the color Kindle itself, suggesting the underlying technology is maturing into consumer hardware applications.
