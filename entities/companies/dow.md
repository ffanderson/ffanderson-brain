### 2026-06-25 — Customer research: software fragmentation

Anthony and Tian spoke with a chemical engineer at Dow who reported using six or seven different software products daily, none of which communicate with each other. The engineer was unable to get [[Microsoft]] Copilot to interface with any of the specialized tools in his workflow, illustrating the integration gap Alluvium aims to address. Fraser cautioned that selling a new operating system to an incumbent like Dow would be very difficult and advised targeting smaller, faster-moving startups instead.
