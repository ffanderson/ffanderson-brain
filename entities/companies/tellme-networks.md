The executive was part of the founding team at Tellme Networks, recruited by Mike McCue after an earlier connection through [[Paper Software]]. Tellme pivoted from a consumer IVR product with an ad-revenue model — which failed because users had low tolerance for ads over phone calls — to an enterprise IVR service targeting companies with high inbound call volumes such as FedEx, UPS, and Fidelity. The company grew to around $110M in ARR and was sold to [[Microsoft]] for approximately $900M.
