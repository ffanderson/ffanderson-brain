### 2026-05-15 — Advisor connection; complementary product
<!-- source_hash: 0fd280b8c7b191a504f46a68624e20e853396d0909d7f618c8c6dba303bcdb0a -->
Cameron Andrews, the founder of Sirona Medical, is an advisor to [[Polaris]]. Sirona builds PACS systems — the software radiologists use to read images — which the Polaris co-founder described as complementary rather than competitive to Polaris's operating system. Andrews's grandfather founded CDI, the first outpatient imaging company, which evolved into [[Rayus]], now the third-largest provider. Andrews was described as a significant influence on Polaris's quality-first positioning as a counter-narrative to private equity roll-up operators.
