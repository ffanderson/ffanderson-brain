### 2026-05-05 — Fund strategy and mandate
<!-- source_hash: 902897b92a06bbcb154ca2bcb1924e22a82117abdef0bf371813ae9d12fa2855 -->
Pattern is a fund-of-funds focused on emerging managers, operating on the thesis that venture has bifurcated and the best absolute returns will come from small, nimble early-stage funds. Pattern generally targets funds of $50M and below, though it will go up to $100M for managers deemed capable of executing at that size. Fund One was just under $50M and backed 18 GPs; Fund Two is targeting $100M with the same strategy and larger checks.
