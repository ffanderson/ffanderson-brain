### 2026-05-13 — Acquired VMS/Jornaya; market position

ActiveProspect acquired VMS/Jornaya on 31 December 2025, making it the sole supplier of TCPA consent-witness infrastructure in the market. Eli noted that ActiveProspect historically competed at the lower, transactional end of the market and never successfully broke into enterprise accounts. Post-acquisition, the company faces the challenge of integrating Jornaya's circa-2011 platform with its own circa-2005 technology, leaving it heavily focused on legacy debt rather than AI-native development.
