### 2026-05-09 — Energy diligence software for PPAs

Volterra was pitched by a Harvard senior (Aaron) as a software platform rebuilding the diligence layer for long-term power purchase agreements, which currently take twelve-plus months to execute through consultants. The product encodes contract clauses, dispute histories, and basis spikes from thousands of past projects into a knowledge model, then surfaces risks when new deals are analyzed. The company had a letter of intent with a data center developer obtained through cold outreach and was in active conversation with Microsoft.
