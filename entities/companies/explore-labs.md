### 2026-05-20 — Founding, product, and shutdown
<!-- source_hash: b0be61af6dcd6936a10198c65b3a7a998da96d93755a0ef4740cf6a63e806a5d -->
Explore Labs was a startup Fraser co-founded with his younger brother focused on AI agents on Ethereum, running for approximately six months before being shut down. The main product, Agent Scan, was a no-code tool for launching trading bots on Ethereum that functioned at a cursory level but was not reliable enough to execute trades with real user funds. The company did secure a signed contract with a Series B AI crypto customer in London, which Fraser cited as a meaningful zero-to-one operating experience. Fraser concluded the market was too early for the product.
