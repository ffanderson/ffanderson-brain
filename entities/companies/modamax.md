### 2026-05-08 — Acquisition structure: clean full buy

[[Fraser Anderson]] advised Jamie that the optimal structure was a clean full acquisition of ModaMax by [[Three Commas]], not a partnership, joint venture, or asset sale. Key terms Fraser recommended pursuing were maximum upfront cash, rolled-in equity upside, a senior title at the new entity, and full assumption of all ModaMax liabilities so no shell company would remain on Jamie's hands. Fraser explicitly cautioned against an asset sale, which would leave residual liabilities and a shell to wind down.
