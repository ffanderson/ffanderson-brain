### 2026-04-21 — Pilot for AI acquisition play
<!-- source_hash: fc2260c03740da7adcd2fce63d09bed8971b488aa3361c2e7b5a7bdac11c4d3c -->
GTCR is being used as the pilot for a strategy in which AI-native versions of legacy SaaS workflows are built externally and then sold back to GTCR portfolio companies at a fixed multiple (roughly 4x). The arrangement exploits an accounting loophole: GTCR can capitalize the acquisition of a new company without impacting portfolio EBITDA, whereas funding internal R&D would reduce it. Fraser noted this makes the structure a win for all parties — the builder, the operator running the startup, and GTCR, which gets a low-risk, splashy-looking acquisition.
