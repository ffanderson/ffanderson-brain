### 2026-05-05 — Program structure and summer 2026 design
<!-- source_hash: 6da76f35d5aa029a926f730d6d55ed1d3a79c33b0204d2227091a685a00ff0d8 -->
Tech Trek is a planned elite summer accelerator recruiting approximately 60 student builders from MIT, Harvard, and Princeton to San Francisco for Summer 2026. The model centers on co-living in rented mansions or Airbnb properties, with roughly 80% of time dedicated to building. The organizers debated but leaned toward paying students, and plan to weave participants into demo days and events including a UBS ultra-high-net-worth family gathering in September 2026 and [[Innovation Endeavors]]-adjacent programming.
