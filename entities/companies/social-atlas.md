### 2026-04-29 — Product vision and positioning
<!-- source_hash: 95a2aa34ed5dc7a9c66d78dfb7dae80e38aa13f7309b05e0ff302c69f749d07a -->
Social Atlas was described as a unified back-office platform for creators and influencers, intended to consolidate legal entity formation, financial tracking, instant payouts, comparative analytics, compliance, and tax forecasting into a single dashboard. The founders likened the vision to what Toast did for restaurants or Shopify for e-commerce, targeting a cited market of over 200 million creators globally operating as small businesses. The AI component was confirmed to be lightweight — context engineering and insight generation only, with no custom per-user agents and no GPU spend anticipated.
↳ source: [[2026-04-29-social-atlas-mvp-scoping-matt-michael-technical-advisor]]
