## Mentions

### 2026-04-28 — Product overview: browser-based AI exam platform
<!-- source_hash: 8eaa43c68560a3d864e9eb74db1c37327f51919a8218447ff7a015ca742dd26c -->
Join My Exam is a browser-based exam platform allowing teachers to instantly create, grade, and provide feedback on exams without downloading additional apps or extensions. Teachers can generate exams via chatbot, upload existing PDFs or lecture slides, and receive per-student analytics; students receive automated feedback to their email immediately after completing an exam. The platform also supports practice exams and tracks student performance across multiple exam sessions over time.
