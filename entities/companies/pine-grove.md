### 2026-06-25 — Formation from SVB Capital assets

Pine Grove was formed as a joint venture between [[Brookfield]] and Heritage (described as an evergreen vehicle) to acquire the bulk of [[SVB Capital]]'s remaining assets following the bank's 2023 collapse. The assets — approximately $10–11 billion — were purchased at around sixty cents on the dollar. A former SVB Capital partner noted he remains an LP in Pine Grove and received a capital call as recently as the meeting date.
