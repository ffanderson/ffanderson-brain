### 2026-04-24 — Ideal pilot customer; economics framing

Louis described Crusoe as the ideal near-term pilot customer because it is vertically integrated—owning land, building, and GPU fleet—and therefore captures the full economic benefit of improved cooling. Chase Lochmiller's public Stanford lecture on data center cost anatomy was cited as effectively laying out [[3DX Thermal]]'s value proposition: Crusoe currently trades water use for chiller energy, spending roughly $500k/MW on chiller capex and incurring significant PUE drag that the technology could eliminate.
