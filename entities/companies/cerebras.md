### 2026-04-24 — Extreme heat load; potential future customer
<!-- source_hash: fe182c655de62fb007b93a7fea9ea2813ee78c8e07d640b8a9f0185d11dff9b5 -->
Cerebras's wafer-scale chip was highlighted as generating roughly 20 kW—an extreme heat load that Nick noted would make a space-based or even next-node version very difficult to cool with existing methods. One participant speculated that Cerebras may face existential cooling problems as it migrates from 7nm to 5nm and 3nm, since area power density rises with each node shrink; this framing positioned [[3DX Thermal]]'s die-etched path as a potential solution. Cerebras is currently in a quiet period ahead of its IPO.
