### 2026-06-25 — Collapse, asset wind-down, Pine Grove JV

SVB Capital had $13.8 billion AUM at the time of the bank's collapse in March 2023. Following bankruptcy, most assets were trimmed and sold; four former partners leveraged a relationship with [[Sequoia]] to form a joint venture with [[Brookfield]] that created [[Pine Grove]], which bought the remaining assets at roughly sixty cents on the dollar. The six months of legally unwinding a decade of work was described by a former head of SVB Europe as among the most miserable professional experiences of his life.
