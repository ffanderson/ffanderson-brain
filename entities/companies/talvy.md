### 2026-06-12 — Pivot to AI talent deployment
<!-- source_hash: ec4fdaf2efe5b3cd3fa10bc7f334586c5be1b58735b184b077521751063d08a0 -->
Talvy was founded by five Harvard CS graduates whom Fraser backed originally to build a video-native LinkedIn. The team pivoted after observing that their high-calibre contacts at [[Anthropic]], [[Palantir]], and similar firms had spare capacity because coding agents were absorbing much of their workload. Talvy now places these individuals part-time at private-equity-owned SaaS companies and 2021-vintage fintech unicorns that need an AI narrative to secure an exit but cannot hire the talent outright.
