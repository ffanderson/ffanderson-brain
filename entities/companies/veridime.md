### 2026-05-05 — Superseded by Claude for spreadsheets
<!-- source_hash: 28914ac965ba1f3f5b54f58656013045f66f3439251f2a268600096e20a129da -->
Veridime, described as an AI-for-spreadsheets company, was cited as an example of bubble-era AI venture risk. Their CTO had argued they were safe from Anthropic competition because Anthropic lacked their concentration of spreadsheet-specific talent — while Veridime was simultaneously building heavily on Claude code. Fraser noted the irony that Claude's own spreadsheet capabilities then superseded them, and contrasted this with a Paradigm CTO who explicitly said their strategy was to distill Anthropic's codebase rather than compete against it.
