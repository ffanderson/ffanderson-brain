### 2026-06-01 — Product and market overview
<!-- source_hash: 1d89317baa1013b109c4b12815021b538f5cd35686aa9214e31e7813a8532dca -->
Florence, co-founded by Harvard rising juniors Sadiq and [[Mark]], is a HIPAA-compliant platform that centralises disability-related records across a person's lifetime and uses AI agents to automate paperwork across sub-verticals including IEPs, 504 plans, and PCAs. The founders cited over 70 million Americans with disabilities as the addressable population and estimated that capturing 5% of the market at $10/month would yield around $210M ARR.
