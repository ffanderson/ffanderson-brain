### 2026-05-15 — Business model: agentic OS + roll-up

Polaris is building an AI-native agentic operating system for outpatient imaging centers as a first phase, with the intention to use operational learnings and proof points to then acquire and operate centers directly. The founding team frames this as a two-stage thesis: prove margin expansion via software, then capture more of the value by owning service delivery. Fraser pushed back on this framing, arguing the acquisition thesis risks obscuring whether the software business is independently viable.
