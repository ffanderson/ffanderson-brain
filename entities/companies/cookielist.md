### 2026-06-01 — Product overview and positioning
<!-- source_hash: 71329700d7b10af2e6b0c9a57c617480bd04240f23388445df2b843cb2924aa6 -->
CookieList is a cookie-less, server-side web analytics tool built by a Harvard student founder, currently being piloted by approximately two dozen teams. Key differentiators pitched were a significantly better UI and lower learning curve compared to [[PostHog]], and server-side integration that technically sophisticated users would appreciate. The founder described two distinct use cases pulling the product in different directions: anonymized aggregate analytics for B2B software companies, and individual user attribution for mid-market e-commerce tracking full funnels from Instagram through to purchase.
