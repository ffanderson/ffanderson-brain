### 2026-05-09 — Fragmented revenue as structural warning

Joe observed that Visa generates revenue across approximately 75 equal increments with no Pareto concentration, which he argued means Visa does not sell products — it operates like a services company akin to Booz Allen Hamilton. He contrasted this with Apple and Google, which each derive roughly 80% of revenue from a single product, and suggested Visa's structure signals stagnation and vulnerability to disruption.
