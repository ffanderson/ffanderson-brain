### 2026-06-17 — Product: airline/hotel upgrade bidding platform
<!-- source_hash: 3f9a8d70f406b19762bb6e98c570b9a2dedc1c350289cf85fa746ab1102669e4 -->
BidUp is a centralized upgrade bidding and price-gap analytics tool that allows travelers to bid on premium cabin upgrades across airlines, hotels, and rail (including [[Amtrak]]). The platform shows the real-time price gap between the traveler's current class and available upgrades, surfaces odds and recommendations, and is designed to integrate loyalty points. Matthew demoed it live against [[Emirates]] flight data.
