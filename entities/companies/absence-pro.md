Absence Pro (Mutual of Omaha's leave management platform) serves as the single point of administration for [[Fraser Anderson]]'s Massachusetts PFL and FMLA claims. It interfaces with the state for wage replacement payments and with [[Linc]]'s payroll to coordinate supplemental top-up pay, and guides employees through the FMLA and bonding leave application steps. No waiting period applies for bonding leave; benefits activate on the first day of leave once proof of birth and a chosen start date are submitted.
