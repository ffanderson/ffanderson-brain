Vigil is positioned as a vertical AI advertising platform that uses reinforcement learning to make margin-aware, multi-signal campaign decisions for e-commerce advertisers. It integrates live business data — margins, shipping costs, inventory, CAC, refund rates, demand signals — with real-time ad signals such as auction dynamics, creative fatigue, and competitor pricing. The platform autonomously generates creatives, shifts budgets, and optimizes across channels around the clock, with an auditable state-action-outcome trail described as a compounding data moat.
