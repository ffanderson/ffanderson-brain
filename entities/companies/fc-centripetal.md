### 2026-05-07 — Platform model and CIO network
<!-- source_hash: 669e7705888fd01bf63af37146bc6af74e0eadf5eadd36564b049af95ade73dc -->
Scott described FC Centripetal as an early-stage enterprise software acceleration platform that runs a weekly survey of 2,500–3,000 CIOs, CTOs, and CISOs, of whom 250+ typically respond, rating whether a startup addresses a real problem and whether they would meet with them as a potential customer or mentor. FC Centripetal takes equity rather than cash from the roughly 15–20 portfolio companies it works with, keeping incentives aligned. Scott also noted a recently announced addition of the CISO of [[Paychex]] to the platform's in-house CIO council.
