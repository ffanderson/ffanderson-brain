### 2026-06-17 — Pivot from finance to healthcare billing
<!-- source_hash: 0d0abeb03f149db39dc38c9c0121633888a4cf6c5d11aa6dea0b83d840380047 -->
Quell pivoted to medical billing automation after two years in finance-focused workflow automation failed to find product-market fit, with buy-side equities investors showing little interest. The founder's father, a practicing doctor, was the inspiration: he had long complained about the difficulty and cost of billing and the money left on the table. Quell started by replacing his father's existing platform, [[Office Ally]], with a proprietary system and now sells that platform to other practices and billing companies.
