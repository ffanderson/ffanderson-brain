### 2026-05-05 — Silent-speech Series A opportunity
<!-- source_hash: 53107293f271e555e1fa6437ef7e89348e2738ac203c77133e8fdc5c310678d3 -->
Tacit is developing silent-speech technology—enabling earbuds or glasses to interpret speech without audible vocalisation—and is raising a $70M Series A at a $350M post-money valuation. The company selected [[Celesta]] as lead investor over competing term sheets; Qualcomm and Amazon are also joining. [[Khosla Ventures]] and [[General Catalyst]] invested in prior rounds and are following on. Commercialisation is planned for 2027, with an obvious acquisition path to Meta, OpenAI, or other hardware-adjacent labs. The firm has a potential $5M allocation and was tasked with preparing a full investment memo.
