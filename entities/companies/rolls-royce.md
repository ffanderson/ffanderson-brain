Rolls-Royce engines were used by Dave as a concrete illustration of how aviation achieves precise exposure aggregation: an insurer can track a specific engine, know it is installed on a particular aircraft belonging to [[Delta]], and follow it globally. Dave contrasted this with the property insurance world's reliance on geocoding, which reduces an entire multi-building hyperscale campus to a single map coordinate.
