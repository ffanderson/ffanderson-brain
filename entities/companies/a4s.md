### 2026-04-26 — Lab research agent; local model deployment vision
<!-- source_hash: dff89411b86d95696a760b2341513ae71c1279ce4d158f02104e1f636a1da7ec -->
A4S (Aging for Science) presented an agent for laboratory research capable of checking Slack history, lab notes, and procurement databases to answer questions like whether a specific antibody had been ordered. A live demo showed the agent recommending a specific microscope model and offering to charge the purchase to an NSF Career grant. The team's broader thesis was that companies controlling local AI hardware — phones, computers, CPU boxes — will become the entry point to a multi-trillion-dollar market.
