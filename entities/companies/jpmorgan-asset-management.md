### 2026-04-01 — Live sub-accounting reference case

[[JPMorgan Asset Management]] was cited as an active parallel-processing reference engagement for the Hansa sub-accounting platform, covering mutual funds in direct sub-accounts and money market funds in a direct omnibus. The platform processed approximately 6.6 million positions totalling roughly $330 million in dividends a full day earlier than the incumbent SS&C system. The project involved a three-phase roadmap: full sub-account omnibus, instant settlement, and eventual convergence of wealth management, asset management, and CIB onto a single platform.
