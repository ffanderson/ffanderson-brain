### 2026-04-01 — Direct assets workflow pain points
<!-- source_hash: d16c2074c6ca0176f765dd9c672391a75da21e0e257f6e4f2041236da75ea34c -->
LPL described a fragmented ingestion landscape for direct assets (mutual funds, annuities, 529s) where the majority of positions arrive through firm conversions rather than the ClientWorks onboarding path. Feeds arrive via NSCC, DTCC insurance/TIPS, and proprietary carrier files into multiple homegrown pipelines, meaning data fixes in one area do not propagate across the organisation. Charles noted that the 'sins of the last thirty years' are visible on conversion files, with missing or malformed CIP fields, incorrect LSCC codes, and incomplete address data being routine.
