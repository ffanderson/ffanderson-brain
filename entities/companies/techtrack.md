### 2026-04-02 — Program concept and structure
<!-- source_hash: cfa6250a746d684b5c1bb078fb684e0e5a5d65488be60869ac7e34ea57e39253 -->
TechTrack is a nonprofit-owned accelerator-style summer program targeting approximately 60 student founders from MIT, Harvard, and Princeton, with plans to expand to the top 20 schools. The model calls for housing cohorts in Bay Area mansions or Airbnbs, devoting roughly 80% of time to building and 20% to curated mentor exposure. Phase one is a summer 2026 West Coast cohort, followed by an East Coast return phase, a January repeat, and ongoing founder calls and IP programming.
