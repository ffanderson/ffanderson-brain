### 2026-04-26 — Agent for managing ML cluster workflows
<!-- source_hash: dff89411b86d95696a760b2341513ae71c1279ce4d158f02104e1f636a1da7ec -->
Alloc Pilot is a repository-attached agent designed to simplify ML cluster job management for researchers. It indexes the codebase, queues and runs jobs on behalf of the user, and if a job fails, attempts non-invasive fixes and reruns automatically. A live demo showed it comparing DDPM and EDM samplers, uploading artifacts to Weights & Biases, and maintaining active traces — all from a conversational interface. The presenter noted this was particularly valuable during multi-day training runs when the researcher is away from the lab.
