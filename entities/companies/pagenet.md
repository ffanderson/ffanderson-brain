### 2026-04-26 — Multi-agent research graph for undergrads
<!-- source_hash: dff89411b86d95696a760b2341513ae71c1279ce4d158f02104e1f636a1da7ec -->
PageNet is a multi-agent system targeting undergraduate researchers who struggle to contextualize their work within a broader literature. It crawls arXiv and ACM, expands via citation graphs, clusters papers using vector embeddings, generates structured summary cards via parallel agents, and adds semantic edges indicating whether papers build upon, share methodology with, or contrast each other. An 'IDEA' button surfaces unexplored and promising research directions, and the graph refreshes every 12 hours.
