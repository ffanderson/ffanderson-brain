### 2026-05-26 — Strategy for competing and co-investing
<!-- source_hash: 488c28d6390f65f37c9609806d768b14990ff7f6ceec36937eb8b3579d7995e4 -->
The team discussed how to position against YC for top recruiting targets, concluding that teams choosing YC over the studio should be ineligible for the tech track program. For YC companies that fit the fund's thesis, the preferred strategy was to participate aggressively in rounds led by a tier-one VC such as [[General Catalyst]] rather than lead post-YC deals, since teams returning to Boston for a lead after YC are likely not top-percentile of their batch. Immediately post-acceptance investments were deemed rare and structurally difficult due to MFN conversion concerns.
