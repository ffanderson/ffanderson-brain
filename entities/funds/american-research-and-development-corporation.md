# American Research and Development Corporation

## Mentions

### 2026-05-07 — First VC firm, founded by Doriot
<!-- source_hash: 260b3f6e6ab1bbde3cce687acfbc80c1bf27e022286233fd7000b69b24079fb0 -->
American Research and Development Corporation was described as the first venture capital firm, founded by Georges Doriot — a French immigrant who served as Quartermaster General during World War Two — to support returning GIs and commercialise technology emerging from MIT's military-industrial complex. The event was held in a Boston house formerly owned by Doriot, lending the evening a direct historical connection to the origins of venture capital.
