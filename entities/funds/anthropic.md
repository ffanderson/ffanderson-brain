### 2026-05-05 — Cited as AI commoditization risk

Both Fraser and his colleague discussed Anthropic as a benchmark for AI commoditization risk when evaluating vertical software companies. The colleague noted that Anthropic is roughly 100x cheaper than comparable incumbent models, making it difficult to identify which vertical software use cases would survive as standalone businesses rather than being absorbed into foundation model capabilities. [[Harvey]] was cited as one of the stronger examples of a vertical AI company that had held its ground.
