### 2026-04-21 — Fund structure and investment philosophy

Mayfield is a 55-plus year early-stage firm with roughly 500 investments, 125 IPOs, and over 230 M&A outcomes. Approximately 70% of investments are the first institutional check into a company, typically pre-revenue and team-stage. The firm runs two vehicles totaling approximately $1.2B: an early fund of around $575M covering inception through Series A, and an early growth fund of similar size covering Series A-prime and Series B.
