### 2026-04-14 — Fund thesis and investment criteria

XPV is Fraser's current fund, focused on AI-tailwind companies — not necessarily pure AI builders, but businesses where AI is the enabling force. The fund favors first-time founders, small tight-knit teams, and companies where the AI component is integral rather than peripheral. Fraser described the ideal profile as 'a few best friends hacking away in an apartment.' The fund plans to invest in ten to fifteen companies during the current year, writing checks of $1–4M, with recent exceptions up to $5M (e.g., [[Starcloud]] with [[Benchmark]]).
