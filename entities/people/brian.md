### 2026-05-12 — Strategic pivot to neuro-annotation/RLBF
<!-- source_hash: ce6831273c4f1c7d2de50b8236ab3622afa9b009f524a28bfa21da9ecc806def -->
Brian described a strategic shift away from building EEG foundation models and consumer-facing applications (e.g., thought-to-text, device control) toward a neuro-annotation and neuro-labeling service for AI training. The new offering uses multimodal biosignals—EEG, EMG, pupillometry, gaze/microsaccades, GSR, respiration, and HRV—to produce dense, millisecond time-locked reward signals. Brian framed this as reinforcement learning from biofeedback (RLBF), targeting frontier labs and enterprise customers rather than consumers.
