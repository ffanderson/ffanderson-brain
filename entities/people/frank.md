### 2026-04-01 — Fund-keeper spectrum and sub-TA explanation
<!-- source_hash: d16c2074c6ca0176f765dd9c672391a75da21e0e257f6e4f2041236da75ea34c -->
Frank articulated the spectrum from purely passive post-trade capture at one end to a full fund-keeper/sub-TA omnibus arrangement at the other, where assets would be held in a single Hansa account with positions tracked underneath, similar to a brokerage sub-ledger. He noted that the sub-TA model carries statementing, tax, and TA-regulatory obligations and suggested the optimal solution for [[LPL Financial]] likely sits in the middle of that spectrum, adding tracking of subsequent investments without full sub-TA responsibility.
