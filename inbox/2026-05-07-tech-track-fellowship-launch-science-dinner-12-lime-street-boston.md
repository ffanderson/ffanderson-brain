# Tech Track Fellowship Launch & Science Dinner — 12 Lime Street, Boston

## Context

A combined fellowship launch event and science salon held at 12 Lime Street in Boston, where organizers introduced 31 accepted Tech Track summer fellows (bound for a San Francisco house) and hosted keynote presentations by neuroscientist Allison Gopnik on the dopamine basis of free will and spontaneous movement, and by Stephen Wolfram on AI, LLMs, and the future of software.
