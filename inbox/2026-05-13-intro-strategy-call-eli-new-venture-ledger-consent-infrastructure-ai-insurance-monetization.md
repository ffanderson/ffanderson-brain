# Intro strategy call — Eli, new venture (Ledger) — consent infrastructure & AI insurance monetization

## Context

Eli, recently departed from the VMS/Jornaya business (sold to ActiveProspect in December 2025), joined Dave and Fraser to discuss his new venture concept — Ledger — an AI-native consumer consent and permission infrastructure platform intended to compete with ActiveProspect and serve as a compliance layer for AI-mediated insurance and lead-gen transactions. The conversation covered partnership opportunities with voice-AI startup Bocara, monetizing OpenAI search traffic for regulated products, EverQuote's strategic priorities, and potential routes to market via white-label carriers and a proposed OpenAI monetization coalition.
