# AI Strategy Briefing — Vestigo/Link Ventures team with Morningstar

## Context

Vestigo and Link Ventures presented to the Morningstar team on AI as an architectural shift, covering their own agent-driven operations, AI-native wealth management models (Savvy Wealth, Farther, Humanity Labs digital workers), emerging compute/infrastructure markets (ORNN), and strategic implications for Morningstar's data, pricing, and value-chain positioning. The meeting concluded with agreement to explore JV opportunities with Humanity Labs and share a shortlist of proposed projects within a week.
